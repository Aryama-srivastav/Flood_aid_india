About Us – Flood Aid India

Floods have been a part of human history for centuries — not just as natural disasters, but as reminders of nature’s immense power. While we may never completely prevent floods — after all, they are often nature’s way of restoring balance — we can reduce their impact. Through preparation, awareness, and coordinated action, we can minimize losses and save lives. Every year, floods affect over 250 million people globally, causing widespread displacement, property damage, and loss of life. Major flood-prone countries include India, Bangladesh, China, the United States, and Indonesia — with India often among the worst affected. But floods don’t exist in isolation. They are part of a chain reaction. Global warming leads to erratic monsoons and rising sea levels. This increases the risk of landslides in hilly areas and urban flooding in cities. The deforestation that causes forest fires also removes natural flood barriers. Earthquakes can reshape land and block rivers, indirectly triggering floods. All these events are deeply interconnected — one environmental disturbance often sets off another.

What We Do

Flood Aid India is a step toward empowering citizens to stay informed and prepared. Our Flood Damage Estimator uses strong logic to: Predict flood damage risk based on your location, house type, and structure age Help you plan, protect, and prepare better in advance Along with this, we provide: Suggestions to reduce risk Access to food and medical aid directories Donation options to help support vulnerable families
